Doodles Ceramics

Doodles is Edinburgh's first informal "Paint your own" ceramic workshop. Decorate mugs, plates bowls, egg cups and more. We offer feet and hand prints for young children, birthday parties or hen parties.

Unleash Your Creativity.

Doodles Ceramics Workshop offers a unique opportunity for everyone to discover their creativity. Come along to Edinburgh's first informal “paint your own” pottery workshop. No experience is necessary and in 3 easy steps you can create your own work of art.

Select your Item: Choose from our own range of white, unglazed pottery items. The range includes mugs, plates, bowls, jugs, vases, tiles, tea-pots, boxes, egg cups and many more.

Pick your Colours: Select from over 80 under-glaze colours. Our studio fee includes all materials: brushes, paints, stencils, stamps, clear glaze and firing.

Create your Original: Paint, splash, splatter, doodle, dab, stamp, stencil, dot and brush your design. Staff will always be on hand to give advice and a design library for more inspirational ideas is also available.

We will then clear glaze and fire your item. Your finished piece will be ready in 4-5 days and will be food and dish-washer safe.
